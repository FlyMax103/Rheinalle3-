Rheingau · Since 1954

Study at EBS.
Live on the Rhine.

EBS Universität für Wirtschaft und Recht — one of Germany's leading private universities — has been part of Oestrich-Winkel since 1954. The campus sits a short walk from Rheinallee 3, making this the most natural place to live during your studies. No commute, no compromise.

Beyond the lecture halls, the Rheingau stretches along the north bank of the Rhine for forty kilometres of vine-covered slopes, historic estates and river promenades. Frankfurt is a direct train ride away. This is not just a place to study — it is a place to live well.
