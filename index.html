Our Story  ·  Nuestra Historia

Calle Pou, Villajoyosa

Tucked into a centuries-old street in the historic centre of Villajoyosa, Calle Pou is a lovingly restored old town appartment where traditional Valencian architecture meets contemporary comfort.

La Vila Joiosa — the joyful town — has welcomed travellers, fishermen, and chocolate makers for over two thousand years. Today, MB Luxury Living brings its warmth and character to those who seek something beyond the ordinary: a private sanctuary, steps from the sea.

AC & Television
How to use the air conditioning and smart TVs in the apartment.
Scroll
AC
Daikin Air Conditioning
Two Daikin Perfera units · BRC4M158 remote · Both floors
Both units are Daikin Perfera wall-mounted inverter AC. The BRC4M158 remote operates in four modes — use the MODE button to cycle through them.
Upstairs
Remote below the unit on the wall
Downstairs
Remote on the nightstand next to the bed
COOL mode — Daikin
The room feels
too warm
Press MODE on the Daikin remote until the snowflake [ SNOW ] symbol appears on the display. Then use the ▲ ▼ arrows to set your desired temperature. 22–24°C is recommended for comfort. The unit will run continuously until the room reaches the set temperature, then switch to a low fan to maintain it.
Snowflake symbol on display Recommended: 22–24°C Hot days · After sun
HEAT mode — Daikin
The room feels
too cold
Press MODE until the sun [ SUN ] symbol appears. Set temperature to 20–22°C using the ▲ ▼ arrows. Note: Daikin heat pump units take 3–5 minutes to begin producing warm air as the system heats up — this is normal. Do not increase the temperature setting while waiting.
Sun symbol on display Recommended: 20–22°C Cool evenings · Winter
DRY mode — Daikin
The air feels
sticky or muggy
Press MODE until the water drop [ DROP ] symbol appears. In DRY mode the Daikin runs the compressor at low power to extract humidity from the air without strongly cooling it. The fan speed is fixed — you cannot adjust it. Temperature can be set but the unit self-regulates. This is the most energy-efficient mode and the right choice on typical humid Mediterranean summer days when it feels sticky but not unbearably hot.
Water drop on display Fan speed fixed — normal Most energy-efficient Humid days · After rain
Always switch off the Daikin when windows or the terrace door are open. Running AC with open windows wastes significant energy and can damage the compressor over time.
